Phases of UHack


Phase 1 : Idea Submission Online

Register and Submit your solution online before 2rd September, 2018 in uhack.in

Phase 2 : Idea Shortlisting

Teams will be shortlisted based on the idea submitted and a confirmation of the same will be sent via team@uhack.in

Phase 3 : Hackthon Phase

Be a part of the 30-hour National Level Hackathon on 26th and 27th of September, 2018 at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, City Campus, Vadapalani, Chennai

HACK DAY-1 (06-09-18)

  • 8.00 AM : Inauguration of UHACK 2018
  • 8.45 AM : Gather For Keynote
  • 9.15 AM : Hack Begins
  • 12.30PM : Lunch Break
  • 01:30PM : Hack Continues
  • 05.00PM : Day 1 Hack ENDS
  • 05.15PM : First Round Of Evaluation [40% Weightage]
    Participants are requested to present the idea of their projects to the judges, briefly explain about their progress for the day and future implementations are to be mentioned.
  • 06.30PM : OFFLINE HACK DAY 1 ENDS

Online Hack

  • 08.00PM (06-09-18) : ONLINE HACK BEGINS
    Participants will have to push the code in github every 1 hour which will be monitored by the UHACK team.
  • 05.00AM (07-09-18) : ONLINE HACK ENDS

HACK DAY-2 (07-09-18)

  • 08.00AM: OFFLINE HACK DAY-2 BEGINS
  • 11.00AM: Lunch break
  • 12.00PM: Hack Continues
  • 12.45PM: Hack ENDS
  • 01.00PM: Final Round of Evaluation[60% Weightage]
    Participants are requested to very briefly explain their idea and are requested to concentrate more on presenting the implementations they have done.
  • 02.30PM : Closing Ceremony
